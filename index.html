About the Author

Phetsy

Phetsy writes fiction that explores the choices people make when the world around them begins to change. Her stories examine divided societies, difficult decisions, human resilience, and the consequences of actions taken during extraordinary circumstances.

Her debut novel, Theend: One Nation, Divided, introduces readers to a nation under pressure and the people forced to decide what they are willing to protect, sacrifice, and become.

This website is the home for news about current books, upcoming releases, future projects, and other updates from Phetsy.
