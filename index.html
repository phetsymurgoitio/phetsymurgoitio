About the Author

Phetsy Murgoitio

Phetsy Murgoitio writes fiction across genres, with a focus on memorable characters, consequential choices, and stories that challenge readers to consider the world from a different perspective.

His first published novel, Theend: One Nation, Divided, introduces readers to a nation under pressure and the people forced to decide what they are willing to protect, sacrifice, and become.
